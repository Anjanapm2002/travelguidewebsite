SPECIAL KERALA FESTIVAL TOURS

VALLAMKALI

Vallamkali, or the snake boat race, is a traditional Kerala sport where teams row long boats in a spirited competition during the Onam festival, showcasing the region's cultural heritage and teamwork.

KATHAKALI

Kathakali is a classical dance-drama from Kerala, known for its elaborate costumes, intricate makeup, and expressive gestures. It combines dance, music, and acting to depict stories from Hindu epics like the Mahabharata and Ramayana.

THRISSUR POORAM

Thrissur Pooram is a grand and vibrant temple festival held annually in Thrissur, Kerala, featuring a magnificent display of decorated elephants, traditional music, and fireworks. It is celebrated at the Vadakkunnathan Temple, attracting thousands of devotees and tourists from around the world.
